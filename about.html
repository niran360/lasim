About LASIMRA

Our Mission

To regulate utility infrastructure deployment through transparent processes, technical excellence, and stakeholder engagement for sustainable development in Lagos State.

Who We Are

The Lagos State Infrastructure Maintenance Regulatory Agency (LASIMRA) is the one-stop shop for the regulation of Utility Infrastructure in Lagos State. Established to ensure orderly development and maintenance of telecommunications and other utility infrastructure, LASIMRA plays a critical role in Lagos State's journey to becoming a smart city.

Our agency operates under the mandate to provide a regulatory framework that ensures safe, efficient, and sustainable deployment of utility infrastructure while protecting public interest and promoting investment in Lagos State.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading infrastructure regulatory agency, fostering sustainable urban development through innovative regulation and stakeholder collaboration.

Core Functions

Permit Processing

Issuance of permits for Right-of-Way, Masts, Towers, and other utility infrastructure

Compliance Monitoring

Ensuring all infrastructure deployments comply with state regulations and safety standards

Stakeholder Engagement

Facilitating collaboration between government, operators, and communities

Data Management

Maintaining a comprehensive database of all utility infrastructure in Lagos State

Our Team

LASIMRA is staffed by a team of dedicated professionals including engineers, regulatory experts, urban planners, and administrative staff who work together to ensure effective regulation of utility infrastructure across Lagos State.

Quality Policy

We are committed to providing excellent regulatory services through:

  • Continuous improvement of our processes and systems
  • Adherence to international best practices in infrastructure regulation
  • Transparent and accountable operations
  • Timely delivery of services to stakeholders
  • Professional development of our staff
